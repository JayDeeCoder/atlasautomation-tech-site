  • You source and close the client — AAG provides the full AI agent infrastructure and handles all ongoing operations and delivery
  • Agent commission: 60% of the client's total first invoice — this includes the monthly plan + any one-time setup fee, paid once upon client activation

  • There are no residuals — you earn by continuing to close. Activity expectations: 50 outbound calls/day or 25 in-person business visits/day
  • You are responsible for the client relationship, onboarding coordination, and initial setup handoff to AAG
  • Full agent agreement provided upon application approval — all terms subject to AAG Agent Policy
